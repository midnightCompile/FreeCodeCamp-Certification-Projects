A Tribute To DaVinci

A glimpse into the life behind the legend.

The Vitruvian Man has become one of Leonardo da Vinci's most reknown works. Created around the year 1487 with pen and ink on paper, in this work he paid homage to the proportional nature of the human being as its parts reflect universal design.
